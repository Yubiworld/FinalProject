About

I am a Danish artist from Kalundborg, living and working in Copenhagen. Nature, witchcraft and Norse mythology have been my main fascinations. I draw with ink, pencil and digital illustration to make my own mythologies, characters and worlds. My dream is clear, I want to work as a concept artist, designing fantasy worlds in collaboration with others. I am a nature person who cultivates herbs and flowers, loves animals and has a lovely "hollow-in-fence dog" called Sally.
